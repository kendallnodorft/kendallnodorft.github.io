About

I'm a multi-passionate creator with a knack for bringing ideas to life. Whether it's designing eye-catching graphics, producing engaging podcasts, or experimenting with virtual reality, I love exploring new creative avenues.

In my spare time, I love experimenting with new photography techniques and taking my beagle mix rescue, Oakley Ray, on hikes with me.

On a more personal note, I have a strong case of Grandpa Syndrome: I may be young, but I act like I’m 80. I enjoy long walks, making par, and driving at just-slightly below the speed limit to optimize my gas mileage. I often spend my evenings watching Wheel of Fortune with the company of my favorite plant, Fernie Sanders, before retiring to bed around 9. I strategically rearrange my body before sneezing to avoid pulling a muscle, and I overly salt and spice my foods just to feel something. I’ve been told my spirit animal is Larry David, which I think is a bit unfair: just last season I learned to snowboard (with the help of my oxygen inhaler), so I should at least be compared to Sylvester Stallone or some other spry septuagenarian. But just because I’m an old dog doesn’t mean you can’t teach me a few new tricks: I’m up to date on all the timely TikTok trends and even learned how to play pickleball.
